Assassin's Creed

Motto

Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad. "Nothing is true; everything is permitted" was the Creed's maxim and primary guideline. The phrase was created during the 11th century by Hassan-i Sabbāh, the first recorded leader of the Assassin Order. Al Mualim taught Altaïr that the maxim commanded the Assassins not to be free, but to be wise..
